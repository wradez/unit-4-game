Welcome to the game Crystal Collector. See the instructions below to elarn how to play.

When you are ready to play, click the button below to get started. You will see a score to achieve, 4 random gems each with their own value, and your score. The point of the game is to click the gems, in whichever order you choose, to get your score equal to the score to achieve. If you can do that, you win! If your score goes over the score to achieve, you lose...!
